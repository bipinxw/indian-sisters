🆓 Zero-Cost Lifelines — Immediate Help Without Money

  1. Protection Officer (DV Act): Every district has one. They are duty-bound to help her file a Domestic Incident Report, take her to a shelter, get medical aid, and file a court application — all for free. Find the number from the local court.
  2. One Stop Centre / Sakhi Centre: A government facility providing medical, legal, police, and shelter under one roof — free.
  3. DLSA (District Legal Services Authority): In every court complex. They provide a full-time free lawyer. If you are below the poverty line (BPL card) or a woman, you automatically qualify.
  4. 1091 / 181 Helpline: Women's helpline, available 24/7 — free call.
